Health monitor — continuous health monitoring via doctor checks.

Runs a subset of doctor checks every 60s (every other reconcile cycle) and writes structured results to /var/lib/nauka/health.json. Logs at WARN for degraded status, ERROR for critical (3 consecutive failures).
